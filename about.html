About Studio ArcLine

Shaping the future through innovative design and engineering excellence

About Us

Founded in 2010, Advanced Architecture Office (AAO) has grown from a small design studio to a leading multidisciplinary firm serving clients across the Middle East. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create architectural solutions that harmonize innovation, functionality, and sustainability. Over the past 15 years, we have completed more than 200 projects ranging from residential villas to large-scale commercial developments and urban masterplans. Each project reflects our commitment to excellence and our passion for pushing the boundaries of architectural design. Today, AAO stands as a trusted partner for clients seeking comprehensive architectural and engineering services. Our integrated approach ensures seamless coordination from initial concept through final construction, delivering projects that exceed expectations.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the leading architectural and engineering firm in the region, recognized for creating sustainable, timeless designs that enhance the quality of life and inspire future generations. We envision a built environment where innovation meets tradition, and where every structure tells a story of excellence and purpose.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality architectural solutions that seamlessly blend aesthetics with functionality. We are committed to understanding our clients' unique needs, exceeding their expectations, and creating spaces that stand the test of time while respecting environmental sustainability and cultural context.
